About

Our company S.H.T.O (stop human trafficking organization) Will offer classes to help educate citizens on the topic of Human trafficking. Human trafficking is a big issue all over the world and it needs to be stopped. By taking our classes you could learn the signs of a person being human trafficked, how to take action if you see these signs, and how to stay safe if you are put into the situation.

You can help with the issue of Human trafficking by taking what you have learned from the S.H.T.O Human Trafficking classes and apply them to real life situations to help a future Human Trafficking victim.
